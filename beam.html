Beam / Channels / Angle Details

Beams are structural elements designed to withstand heavy loads, primarily by resisting bending forces. They come in various types such as I-Beams, H-Beams, and Wide Flange Beams, commonly used in construction for supporting floors, roofs, and bridges.

Beam

Types I-Beams, H-Beams, Wide Flange Beams
Standards ASTM A36, ASTM A992, EN 10025, IS 808
Sizes As per customer requirements (common sizes ranging from 100 mm to 600 mm)
Surface Hot Rolled, Galvanized, or Custom Coatings

Channels

Types U-Channels, C-Channels
Standards ASTM A36, IS 808, EN 10279
Sizes 75 mm to 400 mm (or customized)
Surface Black, Galvanized, or Customized

Angles

Types Equal Angles, Unequal Angles
Standards ASTM A36, EN 10056, IS 808
Sizes 20 mm x 20 mm to 200 mm x 200 mm
Surface Black, Galvanized, or Custom Coatings
